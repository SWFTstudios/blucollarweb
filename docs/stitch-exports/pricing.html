Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any setup fees?

No. Our monthly pricing includes the initial build, setup, and launch of your website and CRM. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you in with heavy upfront costs.

How long does it take to launch?

For The Foundation and The Foreman plans, we typically launch within 14-21 days of receiving your assets (logos, photos, etc.). Custom plans vary based on complexity.

Can I keep my current domain name?

Absolutely. We'll handle the technical work of pointing your existing domain to your new Blu Collar Web platform to ensure a seamless transition with no downtime.
