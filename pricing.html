Frequently Asked Questions

Everything contractors ask before choosing a plan and launching with confidence.

How much does a website cost?

Website builds are a one-time cost starting at $800. Monthly plans are separate and cover ongoing marketing, automation, support, and growth tools.

What is included in the website build?

Your build includes mobile-friendly design, core pages, lead form setup, basic SEO setup, Google Maps and click-to-call integration, analytics setup, copy polish, and launch support.

What is the difference between Essentials, Professional, and Platinum?

Essentials is for getting online fast. Professional adds stronger growth and follow-up tools. Platinum adds the highest level of strategy, support, and market-dominating systems.

Do higher tiers include lower-tier features?

Yes. Professional includes everything in Essentials, plus added growth features. Platinum includes everything in Professional, plus advanced support and strategy.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely. You can start with the plan that fits your current stage and move up as you need more automation, support, and lead generation power.
